Starting September 2006, N52K (N52N) introduced some noticeable revisions made in following areas:

  • Cylinder head cover: Plastic Cover instead of the old alumnium cover
  • Camshafts
  • Crankshaft
  • Stem diameter of exhaust valves increased to 6 mm instead of 5 mm
  • Torsional vibration damper
  • Crankcase ventilation (revised version embedded onto the valve cover)
  • Hot-film air mass meter (HFM)
  • Throttle valve
  • Digital motor electronics upgraded to MSV80
Starting December 2008, a N52K received a new revised cylinder head that fixed the HVA ticking noise.
